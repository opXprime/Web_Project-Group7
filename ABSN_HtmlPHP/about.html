About ABSN Institute of Technology

Our Founding Story

The ABSN Institute of Technology was established on June 15, 2025, by four visionary individuals: Niranjan, Bibash, Sunil, and Achut. The name "ABSN" is a testament to their unity and shared vision, derived from the first letter of each founder’s name—Achut, Bibash, Sunil, and Niranjan. This symbolic acronym reflects their collective commitment to creating an institution that stands for innovation, excellence, and transformative education.

The idea was born during a pivotal meeting on January 20, 2025, when the founders, inspired by their diverse expertise and a shared passion for education, resolved to build a world-class technology institute. Their journey culminated in the official groundbreaking ceremony on April 10, 2025, marking the beginning of a legacy designed to shape the future.

The Founders

Niranjan

A relentless innovator and the driving force behind ABSN, Niranjan envisioned an institution that would not only educate but empower generations. With a deep-rooted belief that education is the cornerstone of progress, he spearheaded the establishment of ABSN to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world impact, ensuring every student emerges as a leader.

Bibash

An expert in technological advancement, Bibash brought a forward-thinking perspective to ABSN. His focus on integrating cutting-edge research into education ensures that ABSN remains at the forefront of innovation, preparing students for a rapidly evolving global landscape.

Sunil

With a passion for sustainable development, Sunil’s vision for ABSN emphasizes creating solutions that benefit society. His commitment to fostering ethical leadership and community engagement shapes the institute’s holistic approach to education.

Achut

A strategist and advocate for accessibility, Achut’s goal was to make quality education inclusive. His efforts ensure ABSN provides opportunities for diverse talents, building a vibrant and equitable academic community.

Why ABSN Was Established

ABSN was founded with a singular purpose: to redefine technological education for the 21st century. The founders, led by Niranjan’s unwavering resolve, sought to address the growing need for skilled professionals capable of tackling global challenges. On March 5, 2025, they formalized their mission to create an institute that blends academic rigor with practical application, driven by Niranjan’s conviction that education must ignite curiosity, inspire innovation, and cultivate leadership.

Our Motive

At the heart of ABSN lies a powerful motive: to empower students to shape a future brimming with possibilities. Niranjan’s vision stands out as the cornerstone—he dreamed of an institution where every individual is equipped to pioneer change, supported by Bibash’s focus on technological excellence, Sunil’s dedication to societal impact, and Achut’s commitment to inclusivity. Together, they aim to build a legacy of transformative education that transcends boundaries.

Future Potential

Looking ahead, ABSN aspires to become a global leader in technological education by 2030. With plans to expand research facilities, forge international partnerships, and launch pioneering programs, the institute is poised to set new benchmarks in innovation. The founders envision ABSN graduates driving advancements in AI, sustainable engineering, and beyond, with Niranjan’s leadership ensuring a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Campus Establishment

The ABSN campus broke ground on April 10, 2025, in a state-of-the-art facility designed to inspire creativity and collaboration. Located in a vibrant academic hub, the campus will feature advanced labs, eco-friendly infrastructure, and dynamic learning spaces, with full operations commencing on September 1, 2025. This milestone reflects the founders’ commitment to providing an environment where ideas flourish and futures are built.
