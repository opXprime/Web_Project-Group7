Our Comprehensive Offerings

At ABSN Institute of Technology, we are committed to providing a world-class education while supporting our students through various services and opportunities. From scholarships to internships, sports facilities to foreign research travel funding, our goal is to ensure our students are well-equipped for success in every aspect of their academic and professional lives.

Scholarships and Grants

Merit-based Scholarships

We offer several merit-based scholarships to outstanding students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance and leadership skills. These scholarships aim to support students in achieving their full potential without the burden of financial constraints.

Need-based Grants

ABSN is dedicated to providing financial assistance to students in need. Our need-based grants help ensure that students from all backgrounds have access to the resources and education they deserve.

Library Services

State-of-the-art Library

Our library is equipped with a vast collection of academic resources, research papers, e-books, and journals. Students have access to modern study spaces and digital resources, making it an essential hub for academic success.

Sports Facilities

Comprehensive Sports Infrastructure

ABSN provides top-notch sports facilities to support the physical development of our students. Our campus includes a gymnasium, basketball court, football field, and indoor sports facilities, promoting a healthy and active lifestyle.

Internship Opportunities

Real-World Experience

We offer valuable internship opportunities with leading tech companies and organizations. These internships allow our students to gain hands-on experience, build industry connections, and enhance their professional skills.

Foreign Travel for Research Funding

Research Grants for Global Exposure

ABSN provides funding opportunities for students to travel abroad for research purposes. These travel grants allow our students to collaborate with international researchers, present their work at global conferences, and gain exposure to cutting-edge developments in their fields.
